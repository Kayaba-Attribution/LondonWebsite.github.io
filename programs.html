Programs

The London Gem & Fossil Society is focused on making our programs fun and educational.
Most of the activities are held outside in groups, some of them are in a theather where speakers give informational talks.
Some activities are: field-collecting minerals and fossils, geology, meteorites, crystal growing, polishing gemstones, carving, metal detecting, and making jewelry.
We are always inproving so new activities are selected from members every 2 months, the last selected was polishing gemstones.
Club meets on the first Thursday from February to June and September to December

Field Trips

Blue Point Mine

We do monthly excursions to the mine, were the owner lets us take 5 pounds for $1
Its one of the society favorites as it gives you garantizied findings
The biggest amrthyst found here was 2 meter tall and over 150 pounds.

Hungry Hollow Quarry

In the Hungry Hollow Quarry is also possible to find crystals but fossils are more common.
We have an unique permission to mine the Quarry as it is not open to the public.
The oldest fossil found here is 65 million years old
